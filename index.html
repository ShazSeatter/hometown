Visit Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

Enjoy culture and food in this vibrant city, the heart of Malaysia.

TOP THREE FOODS TO TRY

  • Nasi Lemak

    Breakfast dish with fragrant rice served with chicken, fried small anchovies, roasted peanuts, cucumber slices, sambal, and hard-boiled egg.

  • Char Kuey Teow

    Any time of day dish, made with flat rice noodles, stir-fried over high heat with light and dark soy sauce, garlic, chilli, egg, fishcake, and beanspouts.

  • Roti Cani

    Breakfast dish made from dough composed of ghee, flour and water. Cooked until flaky and Usually paired with a dish of curry.
